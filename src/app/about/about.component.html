About Us

Our mission is to provide Gryffindor students the best possible way to succeed at Hogwarts. Our application is user friendly, making it easy to use and understand. We are dedicated to making the best product possible so that students are stress free.

Task Management

Nodebucket is a task management application that allows students to manage their student life at Hogwarts. They can easily create, view, and edit tasks to their liking to stay organized. With a flick of the wrist, students can drag and drop tasks to update their status when completed.

Mobile Accessibility

Nodebucket is available on both iOS and Android. There is no need to download the app from the App Store or Google Play. Our website is fully responsive and will work on any device.
